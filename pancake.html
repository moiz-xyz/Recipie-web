Pan Cake Recipie

Pancakes are a popular breakfast dish enjoyed worldwide. They are made from a batter consisting of flour, eggs, milk (or buttermilk), and butter, which is cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan. The batter spreads out and cooks until bubbles form on the surface, signaling it's time to flip it over to cook the other side.

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk (you can use buttermilk for a tangier flavor)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or vegetable oil
  • teaspoon vanilla extract (optional, for flavor)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Batter:
    2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar (if using), baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Mix Wet Ingredients:
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter or oil, and vanilla extract
  4. Combine:
    Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Mix gently until just combined. It's okay if the batter is a bit lumpy; overmixing can make the pancakes tough. Cooking:
  5. Heat a non-stick griddle or frying pan over medium heat. Lightly grease with butter or oil.
  6. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set, about 2-3 minutes.
  7. Flip the pancake and cook until the other side is golden brown, about 1-2 minutes more
    8. . Serve: Serve pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, fresh fruits, whipped cream, or chocolate sauce. Enjoy your homemade pancakes!
